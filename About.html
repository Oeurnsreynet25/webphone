Our Mission & Vision

At PhoneHub, we believe that everyone deserves access to the latest mobile technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge smartphone innovation and Cambodian consumers by providing authentic products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service.

We envision a Cambodia where technology empowers every individual, and we're committed to making that vision a reality by bringing the world's best smartphones to your fingertips.

Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

PhoneHub was founded by a group of tech enthusiasts who saw the need for a reliable smartphone retailer in Cambodia's growing market.

2019

First Retail Store

Opened our first physical store in Phnom Penh, offering hands-on experience with the latest smartphone models.

2020

Online Expansion

Launched our e-commerce platform to serve customers across Cambodia with nationwide delivery.

2021

Brand Partnerships

Became an authorized reseller for Apple, Samsung, and Google, ensuring authentic products with full warranty.

2022

Customer Milestone

Served our 5,000th customer and expanded our product range to include accessories and repair services.

2023

Future Vision

Planning to open additional stores in Siem Reap and Sihanoukville while expanding our online services.
